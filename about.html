About EduSupply

Our journey, mission, and the people behind the passion for education.

Who We Are

EduSupply is a forward-thinking educational company providing top-quality books, digital apps, and learning tools. Our products support learners from kindergarten to college, as well as educators and institutions.

Our Mission

To make high-quality education accessible, engaging, and effective for every learner, everywhere.

Our Story

Founded in 2022 by educators and developers, EduSupply began with a simple mission: bridge the learning gap with innovative, inclusive, and affordable educational resources. Today, we’re trusted by thousands across Africa and beyond.

Who We Serve

We support students of all levels, teachers, parents, and institutions with materials tailored to local and international curricula. Whether you’re in the classroom or learning from home, we have tools designed for your success.

What Makes Us Different

  • 📱 Interactive Learning Apps
  • 💼 Teacher Resources & Tools
  • 🌍 Localized for African Education Systems
